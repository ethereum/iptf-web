## Draft Posts

To create a draft post, you can either:

1. Put it in the `_drafts/` folder (no date needed in filename)
2. Add `published: false` to the frontmatter

Draft posts won't appear on the live site but can be previewed locally with `bundle exec jekyll serve --drafts`.
